Installation Guide

From Stock ROM or Other Custom Rom

1. Have unlocked bootloader.
2. Flash the ROM with Odin (using in the CSC section CSC file, not HOME_CSC).
3. Boot into TWRP and Format Data.
4. Reboot and enjoy OneAI!

From Older OneAI Releases

1. Flash the ROM with Odin using HOMECSC file.
2. Reboot your device and enjoy.
3. If any issues occur, boot into TWRP and Format Data.
4. Reboot and enjoy OneAI!
